About Me

I'm a technology consultant based in Chicago, IL. I help organizations implement modern planning platforms and turn raw data into decisions that actually move the business forward.

My background is a bit unconventional — I studied Chemistry at Grinnell College before pivoting into tech, which means I approach business problems with both analytical rigor and a healthy obsession with getting the details right. I've led EPM/CPM implementations at Spaulding Ridge and previously worked in data, analytics, and AI consulting at BearingPoint.

Outside of work you'll find me at a baseball game, out on a run, or racing a Rubik's cube timer.
